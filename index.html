"Help Us, Help Ourselves"

History of Plant 4 Us Foundation

Plant 4 Us was started in 2019 when Covid-19 hit. It was founded by myself, Brooke Hammer and my older sister. We were looking outside of our house and saw people cutting down trees and we thought we needed to do something about it. So we started Plant 4 Us! A non-profit that plants trees around America. We have been able to get over 50,000 people volunteer in 30 different states.


Plant 4 Us Goals

Here at Plant 4 US, we are looking to keep the population of the trees up around America and we hope to be able to plant over 1 million trees by 2030! In addition, we hope to be able to bring together the largest group of people ever to plant trees in an area that is heavily effected by wild fires.


Services we Provide

Our company has three main services that we provide. Number one being planting of trees. We also love having people come out for community service hours who are able to help us get to our goal of 1 million trees. Finally, we plant an extra tree for each new follower on our Instagram
